Meet Ali-Line

Ali-line is an aluminium lining system that is easy and simple to use. With no need for glue or nails and prefinished so it is ready to go straight up. The unique design allows the lining boards to be removed without damage, giving easy access to services and utilities or to reposition a downlight. Boards can also be installed over insulation to achieve the required R code rating.

Ali-Line is a long lasting, low maintenance, versatile product. Strong and durable because it is aluminium, it looks great and will last for generations. Whether it is for an alfresco ceiling, carport, feature panelling, or commercial application, inside or out, Ali-Line is your answer.
